Procument Services and Contract Support

We provide Industrial Equipments, Spare Parts in partnership with Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM’s) and Technical Partners for both one off order supplies and price agreement long term supplies, all following an integrated approach to procurement servicing.

Our technical teams are involved from the early equipment specification to provide preliminary cost estimates for early RFQ to vendor.

Also, our commercial and logistics teams play a key role to guarantee a hitch-free procurement process from Tender, Factory Acceptance Testing, Transportation & Installation, Commissioning & Start-up and Handover.

Why Choose Pivic

  • Cost-Efficiency: By leveraging Pivic network of suppliers and expertise in procurement processes, this service help clients to obtain products at competitive prices, reducing overall procurement costs.
  • Time Savings: Streamlined procurement processes and efficient logistics from Pivic minimize lead times, enabling timely project execution and reducing downtime.
  • Quality Assurance: With a focus on quality control and compliance, Pivic ensure that clients receive high-quality oil and gas equipment that meet industry standards and specifications.
  • Expertise and Guidance: Clients benefit from the technical expertise and guidance provided by Pivic, allowing them to make informed decisions and overcome challenges in selecting and procuring oil and gas equipment.
  • Reliability and Accountability: Pivic prioritize reliability and accountability, offering transparent communication, Pivic adheres to deadlines, and have a strong commitment to client satisfaction throughout the procurement process.

Pivic Energy is positioned as a major supplier of all types of valves for all major projects procurement needs covering ball valves, gate valves, SDV, check valves, butterfly valves, globe valve etc. We do this through a global network of reputable buying houses and original equipment manufacturers OEMs with whom we have strategic alliances incorporating our well developed supply chain process.

Pivic Energy have a comprehensive inventory, butt weld fittings, socket weld & threaded fittings, and flanges in a variety of types, sizes, schedules and pressures. Our vast in-house inventory featuring domestic, Buy American Act (BAA), and import material means we can ship orders quickly to meet your delivery requirements, and also offer expedited lead times for special or custom requests.

Our inventory includes, but is not limited to, carbon steel, stainless steel, PVC S80, CPVC S80, and specialty alloy materials. Our highly trained staff will help you with your pipe, flanges, fittings, structural shapes, and related components.

We are able to supply carbon steel and stainless steel pipes and tubular to all international Standards such as ASTM, ASME, API, EN and DIN.
